# Om – Sanatan Dharma & Indian Civilization Platform
> The living Guru, civilizational explorer, and Socratic learning mentor.

---

## 1. Project Vision & Mission
Our mission is to build the world’s most comprehensive, educationally sound, and AI-native knowledge platform dedicated to Sanatan Dharma and Indian Civilization. 

### The Problem
Existing digital resources (Wikipedia, Google Search, static blogs, and PDF repositories) isolate and simplify complex, interdisciplinary civilizational knowledge. They fail to connect sacred geography, temporal timelines, textual traditions, and philosophical systems, often presenting them through biased lenses or flat database listings. They also lack educational frameworks for long-term knowledge retention and personal growth.

### The Solution
The Om platform transforms information consumption into Socratic discovery and wisdom. By structuring knowledge as a **hybrid semantic graph**, the platform acts as a living mentor (the digital Guru-Shishya model), dynamically adapting to user locations, goals, and depth levels while providing verifiable citations and dual-chronology tracking.

---

## 2. Core Features & Learning Modes
* **Scripture Reader (`/texts`)**: Word-by-word Sanskrit parsing, commentary overlays, and pronunciation guides.
* **Sacred Geography Map (`/places`)**: Interactive, layered geospatial maps of temple structures, networks, and pilgrimage routes.
* **Historical timelines (`/timeline`)**: Cross-referenced chronological maps tracing Dynastic lineages and astronomical epochs.
* **Philosophical Schools Matrix (`/darshanas`)**: Concept glossary and dialectic debate trees.
* **Universal Command Center**: System-wide Cmd+K search-first command palette.
* **Knowledge Universe Explorer**: Interactive 2D/3D visual graph explorer of lineages and concepts.
* **Personal Workspace**: Private notebook, custom collections, and Svadhyaya reflection portfolios.
* **Knowledge Retention Engine**: Automated spaced repetition (SM-2 based) and active recall testing.

---

## 3. Technology Vision & Philosophy
* **AI-Native GraphRAG**: Grounding Large Language Models within a deterministic RDF/LPG knowledge graph to prevent hallucinations.
* **Predictive Contextual Navigation**: Surface-mounting dynamically relevant items based on temporal (Panchanga) and geographic (Pilgrimage) triggers.
* **Ethical UX (Digital Well-being)**: Replacing dopamine-inducing loops with grace-streaks and offline reflection pauses (*Dhyana*).
* **Universal Learning Framework**: Offering visual, textual, Socratic, and auditory channels mapped to content nature rather than rigid classifications.

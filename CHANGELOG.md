## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-14
### Added
- **P01 – Vision & Strategic Analysis**: Initial vision, problem statement, and long-term strategic positioning blueprints.
- **P02 – Enterprise Product Requirements Document**: Functional and non-functional requirements, cohort rules, and specifications.
- **P03 – Knowledge Architecture & Civilizational Ontology**: Defined hybrid ontology model (RDF/OWL & LPG compatibility), master taxonomies, and multi-chronology schemas.
- **P04 – Human Learning Experience Architecture (HLX)**: Designed Guru-Shishya pedagogical model, spaced repetition retention algorithms, curiosity engines, and digital well-being systems.
- **P05 – Enterprise Information Architecture & Navigation Ecosystem**: Established global navigation patterns, routing tables, and visual Graph Explorer hierarchies.
- **Repository Setup**: Initialized directory structures for documentation, assets, monorepo workspaces, and GitHub configuration templates.
